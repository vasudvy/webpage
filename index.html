About Resonance

Resonance is an AI device for speech restoration. We are making human machine interface to restore speech in patients who lost their voice after stroke or post surgical complications and help them reclaim their self expression.

Our products are aimed to serve our customers with utmost delight and improve their quality of life by a magnitude.
